Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB) has announced that the locations of PSL 2023 matches are Lahore, Multan, Rawalpindi, and Karachi. All matches of Pakistan Super League 2023 will be played in Pakistan and Pakistani Cricket Fans are very Desperate to See their Heroes playing in front of them.

According to the Pakistan Super League 2023 Schedule (also searched as psl match shadol) Announced by PCB, psl start date 2023 is 13th February and a total of 34 matches will be played between 6 teams. The final of PSL 2023 is at Gaddafi Stadium, Lahore.

Previous Seasons of Pakistan Super League:

PSL 2016 was played completely in the United Arab Emirates due to security risks in Pakistan. Islamabad United won the first PSL.

PSL 2017: Peshawar Zalmi won the Cup by Defeating Quetta Gladiators in Gaddafi Stadium, Lahore, Pakistan.

PSL 2018: Islamabad United won the cup in National Stadium, Karachi, Pakistan. Peshawar Zalmi were runner-up.

PSL 2019: Quetta Gladiators won the cup in National Stadium, Karachi, Pakistan. Peshawar Zalmi was runner-up.

PSL 2020: Karachi Kings won their first title in National Stadium, Karachi. Lahore Qalandars were runner up.

PSL 2021: Multan Sultans won their first title in Sheikh Zayed Stadium, UAE. Peshawar Zalmi were runner up.

PSL 2022: Lahore Qalandars won their first title in Gaddafi Stadium, Lahore. Multan Sultans were runner-up.
